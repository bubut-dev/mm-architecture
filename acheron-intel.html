üåê DEX & Uniswap V4 Analysis

Acheron published a detailed technical analysis of Uniswap v4 from a market maker's perspective.

Key V4 Features They Track

  • Hooks: Customizable logic before/after swaps - enables dynamic fees, TWAMM, limit orders
  • Singleton Architecture: All pools in one contract - 99% gas reduction for new pools
  • Flash Accounting: ERC-6909 tokens for internal accounting - more capital efficient
  • Native ETH: 15% gas savings per swap vs WETH
  • CCA (Continuous Clearing Auctions): Fairer price discovery, developed with Aztec
üí° Their View: V4 adoption will be gradual due to hook complexity and large liquidity projects needing migration from v3. As of Sept 2025, V4 handles ~$700M daily volume. They're watching Hyperliquid and Aerodrome as hybrid DEX leaders.

DEX vs CEX Trend

They note a shift toward DEXs for:

  • Cost efficiency - no listing fees (CEXs charge hundreds of thousands to millions)
  • Project autonomy - control over tokenomics
  • Transparency - all transactions on-chain

üìà Their Market Making Philosophy

From their "Fundamentals: Liquidity and Price Discovery" article:

Three Pillars of Liquidity

  • Spread: Difference between bid/ask - narrower = less cost for traders
  • Depth: Capacity to absorb large orders without price impact
  • Uptime: Continuous availability of orders

Four Phases of Token Lifecycle

  1. Pre-Generation: Building relationships with MMs, auditors, exchanges
  2. Primary Listing: First public trading - highest organic volume moment
  3. Price Discovery & Expansion: Finding true market value, expanding to more venues
  4. Cyclical Growth: Inflation ends, steady growth begins
üéØ How They Add Value: They emphasize that choice of market maker significantly impacts a token's journey. They prepare for high volatility at launch, ensure adequate capitalization before market opens, and adjust strategies dynamically. This is their PMM value prop.
