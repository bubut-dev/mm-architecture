üí° Key Architecture Principle
"When in doubt, stop trading."

If the leader can't verify its leadership (Redis unreachable), it MUST stop trading immediately. It's better to miss opportunities than to have two leaders sending duplicate orders.

This is called "fail-safe" behavior ‚Äî when something goes wrong, the system fails in a safe state (no trading) rather than an unsafe state (uncontrolled trading).
