3. Service Lines Deep Dive

PMM - Principal Market Making

Own Capital Loan + Call Option

  • Acheron provides capital via token loan
  • Receives call options as compensation
  • If token price increases, they benefit from options
  • No monthly fees to the project
  • Aligned incentives - MM wants token to succeed
How it works: Project loans tokens to Acheron. Acheron + their stablecoins make markets. They get call options at a strike price. If token pumps, they exercise options for profit. If flat/down, options expire but they still earned from spreads.

DMM - Designated Market Making

Client Capital White Glove

  • Client provides tokens + cash
  • Acheron operates the strategy
  • Monthly fees + profit sharing
  • More of a managed service
  • Risk is on client's capital

Managed Execution

Client-tailored automated agency execution for buying and selling digital assets. For clients who need to execute large orders without market impact.

Command Station (Technology)

Their proprietary trading infrastructure and software, available for licensing with consulting.

Key Feature: Real-time portal for viewing liquidity KPIs. This is their transparency differentiator - clients don't need to hire third-party brokers (like Coinwatch or Glassmarkets) to verify MM performance.

Their View on MM Economics

Critical Point: "Market makers cannot generate volumes. If a market maker claims it can generate volumes, that is a red flag." They can only place limit orders that make markets more efficient and indirectly support volume by enabling trading.

V4 Key Features They Track

  • Hooks: Customizable logic before/after swaps - enables dynamic fees, TWAMM, limit orders, custom oracles
  • Singleton Architecture: All pools in one contract - 99% gas reduction for new pool creation
  • ERC-6909: Internal accounting standard - tokens burned/minted instead of transferred
  • Flash Accounting: Streamlined token transfers, better capital efficiency
  • Native ETH: Direct ETH swaps without WETH - 15% gas savings per swap
  • CCA (Continuous Clearing Auctions): Permissionless auction for fairer price discovery
Their View: V4 adoption will be gradual due to hook complexity and migration needs. As of Sept 2025, V4 handles ~$700M daily volume vs $427M still on V3 for Ethereum. They note Hyperliquid and Aerodrome as hybrid DEX leaders.

8. Options & Tokenomics Research

Key Insight: Tokenomics as Dividend Yield

  • Inflation (emissions, staking rewards): Acts like positive dividend yield ‚Üí depresses option values
  • Burns (EIP-1559, protocol fees): Acts like negative dividend yield ‚Üí increases option values
  • Formula: q_token = inflation_rate - burn_rate
